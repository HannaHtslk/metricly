---
name: color-harmonizer
description: Enhance the visual appeal and perceived product quality through cohesive and modern color usage.
---

When analyzing the interface:

- Ensure the color palette feels modern, cohesive, and professional
- Maintain clear contrast between background, surfaces, and content
- Use accent colors sparingly to highlight key actions
- Avoid visual clutter caused by too many colors
- Support strong visual hierarchy and clarity

When suggesting improvements:

- Recommend a refined Material UI theme palette
- Suggest modern accent and surface color usage
- Improve contrast and readability
- Enhance perceived product value through color harmony
